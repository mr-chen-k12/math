The Mathematics

The dot product can be calculated in two ways:

1. Algebraic Component Definition

$$\vec{a} \cdot \vec{b} = a_x b_x + a_y b_y$$

2. Geometric Definition

$$\vec{a} \cdot \vec{b} = \|\vec{a}\| \|\vec{b}\| \cos(\theta)$$
