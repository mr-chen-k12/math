What is a Polygon?

A polygon is a flat shape with straight sides. The word "polygon" comes from the Greek for "many angles." Polygons are all around us, even though we might not always think about them.

Polygons have:

  • • Straight sides
  • • No curved lines
  • • Sides that meet at vertices (corners)
  • • All points lie in the same plane
  • • Must be closed shapes

Polygons don't have:

  • • Curved sides (circles, ovals)
  • • Open shapes like triangles with curved bases
  • • Intersecting lines or overlapping edges
  • • More than two distinct points where sides meet
