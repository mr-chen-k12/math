Visualizing \( f(z) = z^z \)

This graph uses Domain Coloring. The hue represents the $\text{Arg}(f(z))$, while the grid lines indicate magnitude and phase increments. The function is calculated as \( e^{z \ln z} \) using the principal branch.

Range: Re(-2, 2), Im(-2, 2). The origin \( z=0 \) is at the center.
